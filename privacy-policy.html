1. Important Notice Regarding Text Messaging Data

Mental Capital does not share customer opt-in information, including phone numbers and consent records, with affiliates or third parties for marketing, promotional, or unrelated purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data is kept strictly confidential.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

We use the information we collect to:

4. SMS Messaging & Compliance

By opting into SMS communication, users may receive messages related to:

  • Application confirmations
  • Acceptance notifications

Users may reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency may vary.

5. Information Sharing

Mental Capital does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

We may share information only with service providers necessary to operate the business, such as payment processors, CRM systems, hosting providers, and communication platforms.

Text messaging opt-in data and consent will not be shared with third parties.

6. Data Security

Mental Capital uses reasonable security measures to protect personal information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
