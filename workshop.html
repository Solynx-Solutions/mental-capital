The Framework Behind This Work

This intensive is built on the psychological model outlined in King, Warrior, Magician, Lover by Douglas Gillette and Robert L. Moore.

It defines four core archetypes that shape masculine behavior:

King

Leadership, structure, responsibility

Warrior

Discipline, action, execution

Magician

Awareness, insight, transformation

Lover

Connection, presence, meaning

Growth comes from understanding and integrating these—not operating in imbalance.

When these are imbalanced, they show up as hesitation, overreaction, avoidance, or lack of direction.

What This Intensive Is

This is an 8-week structured process designed to help you understand how you operate at a deeper level.

This is applied self-awareness—not theory.

Each week focuses on identifying and working through a specific pattern so you can apply it in real time.
